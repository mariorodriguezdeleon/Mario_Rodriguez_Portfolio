About Mario

Mario is an innovative Technical Project Manager (TPM @ Hines), Full-Stack Developer and aspiring Solutions Architect. Aside from having a strong technical background, Mario possesses an uncanny ability to translate business needs and requirements to practical solutions. He can bridge the communication gap between the business and the tech team. Simply put, Mario is a strong Information Technology professional with a background in Computer Science, Full-Stack Development and general IT knowledge (Helpdesk, Networking, and Security) who can communicate with both the business and tech teams to develop solutions to meet organizational objectives that deliver value.
